Why We Started VibeCrystals

Late-night coding, chess games that go to move 80, and a heart that still wants to feel grounded, spiritual, and aligned.

Crystals for the New-Age Creative

We grew up on city lights, laptops, social media, and side hustles. At the same time, we felt called to something deeper — ritual, spirituality, and intentional living.

VibeCrystals is our bridge between those worlds: modern life and ancient stones; high-tech and high-vibe.

The First Setup

One amethyst by the keyboard. A little citrine by the camera. A thrift-store chess set on the desk. That was the first VibeCrystals “studio”. From there, the idea never left.

What We Stand For

Ethical Sourcing

We work only with suppliers who respect fair wages, safe working conditions, and the land crystals come from.

Energy Over Aesthetic

A crystal doesn’t need to be Instagram-perfect to be powerful. We choose pieces for how they feel as much as how they look.

Education & Empowerment

Every order includes simple, no-gatekeeping guides so you actually know how to use what you buy.

Built by a Real Human

This brand is coded, filmed, and shipped by one person building their dream life — just like you. Every order supports that journey.
